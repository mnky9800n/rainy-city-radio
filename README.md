
# rainy-city-radio

rainy-city.com now has it's own rainy city radio! Broadcasting live on Youtube, and on 99X (99.7FM) inside of rainy-city.com. Rainy-city-radio uses ffmpeg to stream to youtube live.

rainy-city-radio has DJ Jennifer! DJ Jennifer is the hero of Streets of Rainy-city.com fighting evil satan worshippers, but her day job is a DJ at the radio station. She plays rainy-city vibes electro rain music and tells rainy city stories over the air waves when its not a commercial break.

Jennifer's voice comes from eleven labs API, it includes pre-recorded spots that play regularly, as well as periodic replies to comments in the chat.

The music comes from mp3s on the server.
